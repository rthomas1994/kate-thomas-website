Kate Thomas

Voice Over

Storyteller

Performer

Kate Thomas is a professional voice over artist, actress, and teacher. She is a passionate storyteller who along with her 15 years of acting experience, earned a Bachelor’s degree in Theatre Arts from Boston College.

Kate has an extensive background in acting, teaching and production with experience working for companies like Stacey Mindich Productions on Broadway and The Walt Disney World Company. She uses her training as both an actor and educator to specialize in eLearning, animation, commercials, video games, singing, and promos.

Training & Experience

Boston College - Kate earned her B.A. in Theatre Arts from Boston College. She spent most of her spare time in rehearsal, both for the University Chorale and for the mainstage and “after hours” theatre productions.

Classes - She took classes in all disciplines, such as French, Directing, Acting, and Theatre for Youth. She spent summers training with various voice coaches, attending master classes, and about a month learning in London for a Contemporary Theatre and Drama course.

Disney - At Disney, she honed her improv skills working with different iconic characters. Being the voice of those characters and bringing them to life through storytelling was pure magic. She also attended conferences and seminars on creativity, cast engagement, and one's impact on others.

Teaching - While teaching and directing children’s theatre for mainstage performances, she got her certification in early childhood education. Due to her years of experience working with young audiences, she specializes in educational language.
