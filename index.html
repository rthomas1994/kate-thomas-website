Training & Experience

During school I performed in as many mainstage and “After Hours” productions as possible, and I trained at Boston College with Patricia Riggin, John Houchin, Luke Jorgensen, Crystal Tiala, and Scott T. Cummings in:

  • Directing
  • Acting: Voice
  • Body
  • Text
  • Youth Theatre

I also spent time working for Disney and diving into directing. It was during that time I fell in love with being the voice of these iconic characters. Being able to bring these characters to life through storytelling was spellbinding and it felt like magic.
